About Beat the Timer

Beat the Timer was created to make chores less of a battle and more of a game. Because nobody should have to beg their kids to tidy up.

  • Spin the wheel to get a random chore
  • The countdown timer starts automatically
  • Race to finish before the timer runs out
  • Beat it — celebrate! Miss it — try again!
